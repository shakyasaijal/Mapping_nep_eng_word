kta haru skul gaeraheko chan
ek kta skul gaeraheko cha
ek kta tabul maa basiraheko cha
ek kta geet gaaeraheko cha
yo geet ramro ho
uni ramro kt hun
ma skul jaanchu
kta haru chaur maa kheloraheka chan
ek kt chaur maa basiraheko cha
uni kitab padchin
ma sikshyek hu
ek sikshyek yo path padhaucha
yo skul sanga ramro chaur cha
yo geet dherai prakhyat cha
u skul maa padhaucha
u sanga kitab cha
u prakhyat cha
yo path yo kitab maa cha
